The horror...

I made this site circa 2000 (in high school) as a tribute to my all-time then favorite rapper, Eminem. Later, after some complaints perhaps, it evolved into a kind of self-promotional / favorites / shout-outs page. The green on black is especially reminiscent of early website, though I used the modern san-serif font Verdana in my CSS to hip it up.

This is pre-Google, hence the Yahoo search (remember when it was cool to put a search gadget on your site?)

Note that practically none of these opinions mirror my current values. This is only an archive.
